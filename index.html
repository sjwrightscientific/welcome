I am a science, engineering, and business development consultant with expertise in semiconductor and thin film processes. Who I Am

I graduated in 2010 with a PhD in Physics from Cambridge University in the UK. I am now a Permanent Resident of the US living in New York. For the last 10+ years I have been working for advanced process equipment manufacturers in a range of roles including Process Scientist, Project Engineer, Applications Engineer, and Product Marketing Manager. My skills include thin film deposition equipment maintenance and operation, process engineering, customer support, product management, sales project management, marketing, and business development.
